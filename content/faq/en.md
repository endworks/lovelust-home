# LoveLust FAQ

## Is my data really private?

Yes. Your sexual activity, partner, medication and health data is stored **locally on your device**, in encrypted storage, and is never sent to LoveLust servers, never sold, and never shared with advertisers. It is shared with Apple Health or Health Connect only if you turn that integration on. See the [Privacy Policy](/privacy) for full detail.

## Does anything leave my device?

Your intimate data does not. The app does send limited, non-sensitive data to our backend (an internal account identifier, your language, your notification token, app version, and your communication preferences) to deliver notifications you asked for. We also use anonymous analytics (off until you consent) and crash diagnostics. None of this includes your activity, partners, medication or health data.

## What is the encrypted cloud backup?

It's an optional premium feature. If you enable it, your data is encrypted **on your device** with a key derived from a passphrase you choose, then uploaded to **your own** Google Drive or iCloud. We never receive your backup or your passphrase.

## What happens if I lose my backup passphrase?

We cannot recover it, and we cannot decrypt your backup without it; that is the point of the encryption. If the passphrase is lost, the backup cannot be restored. Store it somewhere safe.

## What happens if I lose my phone?

If you did not enable cloud backup, your data lived only on that device and is gone. We recommend enabling encrypted cloud backup in Settings so you can restore on a new device.

## How do I delete all my data?

In the app: **Settings → Storage → Clear all Data** erases everything on the device. Uninstalling the app also removes local data.

**Important:** if you used cloud backup, clearing local data or uninstalling does **not** delete the backup. To remove it, go to **Settings → Cloud Storage**, sign out, and delete the remote backup. We keep it until you do, so you can restore after reinstalling.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

Cancel anytime in your App Store or Google Play subscription settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Deleting the app does **not** cancel your subscription. Refunds are handled by Apple or Google under their policies.

## Do I have to be 18?

Yes. LoveLust is intended only for users 18 or older.

## What are my GDPR rights?

You can access, correct, delete, export, and restrict your data, and withdraw consent (GDPR Art. 15–21). Most of this you can do directly in the app: export via **Settings → Storage → Export Data**, delete via the steps above, and withdraw analytics/sensitive-data consent in **Settings → Privacy** without deleting anything.
